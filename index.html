About

I am Joshua Lubinda, a Computer Science student with a strong interest in front-end development and user interface design. I enjoy translating ideas into polished, responsive web interfaces that feel intuitive from the first interaction. My approach combines technical implementation with design thinking, so I can move from wireframes and visual planning to clean, maintainable code. Through coursework and personal practice, I have focused on building solid fundamentals in web technologies while also improving how I structure layouts, spacing, and typography for readability.

Beyond coding, I pay close attention to user journeys, accessibility, and visual hierarchy because good products should be easy to understand for everyone. I regularly prototype concepts in Figma before implementing them in the browser, which helps me test design decisions early and communicate ideas clearly. My long-term goal is to grow into a product-minded software engineer who can design and develop complete web experiences that are elegant, efficient, and reliable.
